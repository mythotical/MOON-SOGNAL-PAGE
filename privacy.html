Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Moon Signal is a paid browser extension and marketing website. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

Summary

  • We do not sell personal data.

  • We aim to collect the minimum data required for operation and support.

Information we may collect

  • Account / billing data: processed by Stripe (e.g., email, payment method). We do not store raw card details.

  • Basic diagnostics: limited logs for reliability (e.g., validation success/failure, timestamps).
  • Optional Wallet Activity inputs: only if you enable the feature in the extension.

How we use information

  • Provide license validation and feature gating.
  • Prevent abuse (e.g., excessive key sharing).
  • Operate support and troubleshoot issues.

What we do not do

  • We do not provide financial advice.
  • We do not guarantee outcomes or performance.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, legal obligations, and abuse prevention.

Your choices
