Overview

Moon Signal is a Chrome extension that provides decision-support signals and optional alerts. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • License key validation: When you paste a license key, the extension sends it to our backend to verify your subscription tier.
  • Basic telemetry (optional): We may collect minimal operational data needed to keep the service running (e.g., API error rates). We do not sell personal data.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your passwords.
  • We do not read your private messages or emails.
  • We do not sell your data.
