FAQ

Clear answers, no marketing fog.

Is Moon Signal financial advice?

no
Moon Signal is an informational tool. It provides risk-aware context to support your decisions. You are responsible for execution and risk.

How do I activate after purchase?

license key
After checkout, you receive a license key. Paste it into the extension’s Settings → License section to unlock your plan.

What does ENTER / BLOCKED / RUG mean?

states
ENTER indicates conditions align for execution; BLOCKED means conditions do not qualify; RUG indicates elevated rug risk based on on-chain / distribution behavior. The “why” and invalidation are shown in-app.

Do you track my wallet?

privacy
Wallet Activity (if enabled) is clearly labeled and separate from core signals. See the Privacy Policy for details on what data is processed.

Can I cancel anytime?

billing
Yes. Your subscription can be managed through Stripe. Access remains active through the end of the billing period.
