FAQ

Clear answers. No hype.

Does Moon Signal guarantee profits?
No. Moon Signal is decision support. It helps reduce obvious risk, but markets can’t be guaranteed.
Is this financial advice?
No. Signals and risk context only. You are responsible for your trades.
How does access work?
After checkout, you receive a license key tied to your plan. The extension validates it with the backend.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Your subscription ends at the end of the billing period. Your license stops validating when it expires.
