FAQ

Clear answers. No hype.

Does Moon Signal guarantee profits?
No. Moon Signal is decision support. It helps you act with discipline and avoid obvious risk, but markets can’t be guaranteed.
Is this financial advice?
No. Moon Signal provides signals and risk context. You are responsible for your trades.
What happens if I cancel?
Your subscription ends at the end of the billing period. Your license will stop validating once it expires.
How do license keys work?
After checkout, you receive a license key tied to your plan. The extension validates it with the backend to unlock tier features.
