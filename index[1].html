FAQ

Clear answers. No hype.

Is this financial advice?
No. Moon Signal provides signals and risk context only. You are responsible for your trades.
Does Moon Signal guarantee profits?
No. Markets are risky and outcomes can’t be guaranteed. Moon Signal is decision support.
What happens if I cancel?
Your subscription ends at the end of the billing period. Your license will stop validating after expiry.
How do license keys work?
After checkout, you receive a license key tied to your plan. The extension validates it with the backend to unlock features.
