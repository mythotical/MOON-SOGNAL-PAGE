Overview

Moon Signal is a Chrome extension that provides decision-support signals and optional alerts. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • License key validation: When you paste a license key, the extension sends it to our backend to verify your tier.
  • Operational logs: Minimal server logs may be used to keep the service reliable (e.g., error rates).

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your passwords.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not read private messages.

Third-party services

Payments are processed by Stripe. Their privacy policy applies to payment information.
